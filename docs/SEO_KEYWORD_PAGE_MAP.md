## SEO Keyword → Page Map (Astro)

Purpose: keep one clear **primary intent** per page, avoid duplicate/doorway content, and ensure internal links reinforce topical authority.

### Brand + trust verification (high intent)
- **`/official-booking`**: “official booking”, “verified booking”, “authentic booking”, scam prevention CTA.
- **`/official`**: “official website”, “is it genuine/legit”, trust verification, contact validation.
- **`/contact-number`**: “official contact number”, “helpline number”, WhatsApp number verification.
- **`/booking`**: “book room”, “room booking”, “advance booking”, booking steps and FAQs.

### Room intent (transactional)
- **`/rooms/2-bed-ac`**: “2 bed AC room”, “double room AC”, price + amenities.
- **`/rooms/2-bed-non-ac`**: “2 bed non AC room”, “budget room”, price + amenities.
- **`/rooms/3-bed-ac`**, **`/rooms/3-bed-non-ac`**: family / triple sharing intent.
- **`/rooms/4-bed-ac`**, **`/rooms/4-bed-non-ac`**: group / yatri mandal intent.
- **`/rooms/deluxe-room`**, **`/rooms/family-suite`**: premium intent.

### Location intent (local SEO)
- **`/location/near-ram-mandir`**: “near Ram Mandir”, walking distance, directions.
- **`/location/karsewakpuram`**: locality targeting and NAP reinforcement.
- **`/location/ayodhya`**: broad Ayodhya stay intent (hub).
- **`/location`**: directory/hub (internal linking).

### Informational authority (blog)
- **`/blog`**: listing page (paginated) for internal linking to guides.
- **`/blog/<slug>`**: long-tail queries (travel, booking verification, routes, seasonality).

### Legal trust pages (E‑E‑A‑T support)
- **`/privacy-policy`**, **`/terms-of-service`**, **`/cancellation-refund`**: compliance + trust reinforcement.

### Internal linking rules (quick)
- Every blog post should link to **one** primary conversion page: `(/official-booking)` or `(/booking)` based on intent.
- Room pages should link back to `(/booking)` and to 2–3 related room types (avoid keyword cannibalization).
- Location pages should link to `(/booking)` and to the nearest relevant room pages.
