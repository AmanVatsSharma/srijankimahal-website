# Allahabad (Prayagraj) to Ayodhya Journey Guide - Train, Bus & Stay

Allahabad (Prayagraj)—home to Sangam—and Ayodhya are both major pilgrimage sites. This guide covers the journey between them and staying at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Allahabad/Prayagraj

**Stations**: Prayagraj Junction (PRYJ) / Allahabad Junction (ALD) to Ayodhya Junction (AY)  
**Duration**: Approximately 3–5 hours  
**Frequency**: Multiple trains daily

Check IRCTC for current schedules. Book in advance for festival dates.

## Bus from Prayagraj

**UPSRTC and private buses**: Regular services to Ayodhya  
**Duration**: 4–6 hours  
**Terminal**: Ayodhya bus stand or near Ram Mandir area

## From Ayodhya to Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is in Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir. From Ayodhya Junction or bus stand:
- Auto/rickshaw: 15–25 minutes
- Walking: Possible with light luggage; see [location from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction)

## Combined Sangam–Ayodhya Itinerary

1. **Prayagraj**: Sangam, Kumbh area (if applicable)
2. **Travel**: Train or bus to Ayodhya
3. **Ayodhya**: Stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, Ram Mandir darshan

Book your Ayodhya room in advance via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact](/contact-number). Check [rooms](/rooms) and [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).
