# Ayodhya Accessibility Features: Wheelchair and Special Needs Guide

Ayodhya welcomes pilgrims of all abilities, and understanding accessibility features helps ensure a comfortable and meaningful pilgrimage experience for visitors with mobility challenges or special needs. This comprehensive guide covers wheelchair accessibility, special accommodations, and inclusive planning strategies to help all pilgrims make the most of their Ayodhya visit.

## Accessible Accommodation Options

When booking accommodation, communicate your specific accessibility needs clearly. Many accommodations offer accessible rooms with features like wheelchair-accessible entrances, grab bars, and accessible bathrooms. When booking through the [official booking portal](/official-booking), specify your requirements and verify accessibility features. Contact the [official contact number](/contact-number) to discuss specific needs and confirm that your chosen accommodation can meet them.

## Wheelchair Access and Mobility Considerations

Wheelchair accessibility varies across different areas of Ayodhya. Some temples and ghats may have steps or uneven surfaces that present challenges. Research accessibility at specific sites you plan to visit. If staying at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, check the [location guide](/location) to understand terrain and accessibility of nearby attractions. The [location guide near Ram Mandir](/location/near-ram-mandir) can help you plan accessible routes to the temple.

## Accessible Room Features

Accessible rooms typically include features like wider doorways, roll-in showers or accessible bathtubs, grab bars, lower light switches and outlets, and accessible furniture heights. When reviewing the [rooms page](/rooms), look for accessibility information or contact the [official contact number](/contact-number) to inquire about specific accessible room features. Verify these details before booking to ensure your comfort and safety.

## Transportation and Mobility Assistance

Local transportation options vary in accessibility. Some auto-rickshaws and taxis may accommodate wheelchairs, but it's best to arrange accessible transportation in advance. If arriving by train at Ayodhya Junction, check the [location guide from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction) for accessible transport options. Plan your transportation needs when booking accommodation through the [official booking portal](/official-booking).

## Temple and Darshan Accessibility

Temple accessibility varies, and some areas may have restrictions or challenges for wheelchair users. Contact temple authorities in advance to understand accessibility options and any special arrangements available. Some temples offer special darshan arrangements for visitors with mobility challenges. Plan your temple visits considering accessibility and allow extra time for navigation.

## Special Dietary and Medical Needs

If you have special dietary requirements or medical needs, communicate these when booking accommodation through the [official booking portal](/official-booking). Many accommodations can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice. For medical needs, verify proximity to medical facilities using the [location guide](/location). Keep medical information and emergency contacts easily accessible during your visit.

## Planning and Preparation

Thorough planning ensures a smoother experience. Book accommodation well in advance through the [official booking portal](/official-booking) to secure accessible rooms, which may be limited. Verify all accessibility features through the [official contact number](/contact-number) before your visit. Bring necessary mobility aids and medical supplies, and have backup plans for any accessibility challenges you might encounter.

## Support and Assistance

Don't hesitate to ask for assistance when needed. Accommodation staff are typically willing to help with accessibility needs. When booking through the [official booking portal](/official-booking), communicate your requirements clearly. Traveling with a companion can provide additional support, but many accommodations and local services can assist solo travelers with special needs.
