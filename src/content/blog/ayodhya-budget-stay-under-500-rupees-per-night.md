# Ayodhya Budget Stay Under 500 Rupees Per Night (Options & Janaki Mahal)

Ayodhya offers various accommodation options. Here's how to plan a budget stay and what to expect at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Budget Options in Ayodhya

- **Dharmshalas**: Often the most affordable; donation or nominal charge
- **Shared rooms**: Lower cost when sharing with family or group
- **Non-AC**: Typically cheaper than AC—see [AC vs non-AC guide](/blog/ayodhya-dharmshala-ac-vs-non-ac-when-to-book)

## Sri Janaki Mahal Trust Pricing

The trust offers rooms at reasonable rates. **Exact prices** vary by room type and season. Check [room types and prices](/rooms) and contact the [official number](/contact-number) for current rates. Meals are included—adding value.

## Under ₹500 Per Night

- **Shared accommodation**: Some dharmshalas offer beds at lower rates
- **Non-AC, multiple sharing**: Often most economical
- **Off-peak**: Rates may be lower outside festivals

**Sri Janaki Mahal Trust** may have options that fit budget pilgrims—inquire when you [book](/official-booking).

## Avoid Fake Budget Offers

Scammers promise "cheap" rooms and take money. Book only through [official channels](/official-booking). See [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams).
