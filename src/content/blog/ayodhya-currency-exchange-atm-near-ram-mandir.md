# Ayodhya Currency Exchange & ATM Near Ram Mandir (Pilgrim Guide)

Managing cash during your Ayodhya pilgrimage? Here's where to find ATMs and what to expect near Sri Janaki Mahal Trust and Ram Mandir.

## ATMs in Ayodhya

- **Near Ram Mandir / Karsewakpuram**: Several bank ATMs serve the area
- **Ayodhya Junction**: ATMs at or near the station
- **Main market areas**: Additional ATMs for backup

**Carry some cash**—ATMs can run out during festivals. Plan for purchases, transport, and small donations.

## UPI and Digital Payments

UPI is widely accepted in Ayodhya—shops, autos, and many dharmshalas. Sri Janaki Mahal Trust accepts payments as per their policy—see [payment options](/blog/janaki-mahal-trust-payment-options-advance-booking). Avoid [UPI scams](/blog/ayodhya-upi-payment-scam-red-flags-booking).

## Currency Exchange

- **Banks**: In Ayodhya town for forex (if you carry foreign currency)
- **For Indian visitors**: ATMs and UPI are sufficient

## Near Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

The trust is in Karsewakpuram. ATMs are within walking or short auto distance. Withdraw before peak darshan hours to avoid queues.
