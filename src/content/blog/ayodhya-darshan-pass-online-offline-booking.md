# Ayodhya Darshan Pass - Online & Offline Booking (Ram Mandir Entry)

Ram Mandir darshan may require a pass or token. Here's an overview of the process and how your stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust helps.

## Darshan Pass Process

The exact process can change. Currently:
- **Online**: Booking may be available via the official Ram Mandir / government portal
- **Offline**: Counters or token system at the temple
- **Walk-in**: Sometimes allowed; subject to capacity and day

**Check the latest** on the official Ram Mandir website or with temple authorities when you arrive.

## Tips for Pilgrims

- **Book early**: If online slots exist, book as soon as they open
- **Carry ID**: Proof of identity is usually required
- **Reach on time**: Arrive before your slot if time-bound
- **Dress code**: Follow [cultural etiquette](/blog/ayodhya-cultural-etiquette-dress-code-guide)

## Advantage of Janaki Mahal Stay

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is **walking distance** to Ram Mandir. You can:

- Rest before and after darshan
- Use [early morning darshan tips](/blog/ayodhya-early-morning-darshan-tips-ram-mandir)
