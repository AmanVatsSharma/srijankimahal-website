# Ayodhya Dham Yatra - Sri Janaki Mahal as Base Stay (Itinerary Guide)

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust's location near Ram Mandir makes it an ideal base for a multi-day Ayodhya Dham yatra. Here's a practical itinerary guide.

## Why Sri Janaki Mahal as Base?

- **Walking distance to Ram Mandir**: Saves time and transport costs
- **Meals included**: Breakfast, lunch, dinner—focus on darshan, not food hunt
- **Comfortable rooms**: AC and non-AC options—see [rooms](/rooms)
- **Trust-managed**: Safe, verified, and scam-free when booked via [official channels](/official-booking)

## Sample 2-Day Itinerary

**Day 1**:
- Arrive, check in at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust
- Ram Mandir darshan (morning or evening)
- Saryu Ghat visit, local temples

**Day 2**:
- Early morning aarti at Ram Mandir (if applicable)
- Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan, other temples
- Check out, departure

## Sample 3-Day Itinerary

**Day 1**: Arrival, check-in, Ram Mandir darshan  
**Day 2**: Full temple circuit—Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan, Saryu Ghat, Nageshwarnath  
**Day 3**: Morning darshan, checkout, travel to next destination

Book in advance via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact](/contact-number). For festival dates, use the [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).
