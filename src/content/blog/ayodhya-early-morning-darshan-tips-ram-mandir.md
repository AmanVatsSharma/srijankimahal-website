# Ram Mandir Early Morning Darshan Tips (Ayodhya) — Less Crowd, Better Flow

Early morning darshan can feel calmer and easier—especially for families, elders, and devotees who want a peaceful experience.

## Why morning darshan works well

- Cooler weather (better walking comfort)
- Often smoother queue movement
- More time left in the day for rest or another darshan

## What to carry (simple checklist)

- Valid ID proof
- Water (as needed)
- Minimal belongings (avoid large bags)
- Comfortable footwear (follow temple rules where required)

## FAQs

### Is morning darshan better than evening?

Both are good. Morning is often cooler and easier for seniors; evening can be peaceful too depending on the day.
