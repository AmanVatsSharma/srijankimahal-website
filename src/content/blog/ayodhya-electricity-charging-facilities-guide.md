# Ayodhya Electricity and Charging Facilities Guide

Managing your electronic devices and ensuring access to charging facilities is important for a smooth pilgrimage experience in Ayodhya. Whether you need to charge your phone, camera, or other devices, understanding electricity availability and charging options helps you stay connected and capture your spiritual journey. This guide covers electricity facilities, charging options, and tips for managing your devices during your Ayodhya visit.

## Electricity Availability and Power Supply

Ayodhya has reliable electricity supply, though power cuts can occasionally occur, especially during peak demand periods or adverse weather. Most accommodations provide power backup through generators or inverters to ensure continuous electricity. When booking accommodation, inquire about power backup facilities to ensure your devices can be charged reliably.

## Accommodation Charging Facilities

Most accommodations provide charging points in rooms for guests' convenience. If staying at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, check the [rooms](/rooms) available and their charging facilities when booking through the [official booking portal](/official-booking). Verify charging facilities and power backup through the [official contact number](/contact-number) before your visit. Accommodations typically provide multiple charging points and may offer charging stations in common areas.

## Power Backup and Generator Facilities

Many accommodations maintain generator or inverter backup to ensure continuous power supply during outages. This is especially important for charging devices and maintaining comfort. When booking, inquire about backup power capacity and duration. Accommodations like Sri Janaki Mahal Trust maintain proper facilities, as detailed in their [official information](/official). Power backup ensures your devices remain charged even during temporary outages.

## Mobile Phone Charging

Mobile phones are essential for navigation, communication, and photography during your visit. Ensure your accommodation provides charging facilities in your room. Carry a portable power bank as backup for extended temple visits or when charging points aren't immediately available. Many accommodations offer charging stations in common areas or reception areas for guest convenience.

## Camera and Electronic Device Charging

If you're carrying cameras or other electronic devices, ensure you have appropriate chargers and adapters. Most accommodations provide standard power outlets compatible with Indian plugs. Verify charging facilities for your specific devices when booking accommodation. Plan your [itinerary](/blog/ayodhya-2-day-itinerary-ram-mandir-stay) to include charging time for your devices, especially if you're taking extensive photos or videos.

## Public Charging Facilities

Some public areas, cafes, and restaurants may offer charging facilities, though availability varies. Temple premises may have limited charging options, so ensure your devices are fully charged before temple visits. If you need charging during your visit, check with your accommodation about nearby options. The [location guide](/location) can help identify facilities in your area.

## Power Bank and Portable Charging Solutions

Carrying a portable power bank is highly recommended for extended temple visits or when charging points aren't immediately available. Power banks allow you to charge devices on the go, which is especially useful during long darshan queues or full-day sightseeing. Choose a power bank with sufficient capacity for your device needs and ensure it's fully charged before your visit.

## Voltage and Plug Compatibility

India uses 230V electricity with Type C, D, and M plug sockets. If you're traveling from abroad, bring appropriate adapters for your devices. Most modern electronic devices support multiple voltage ranges, but verify compatibility before use. Accommodations typically provide standard Indian power outlets, and some may offer international adapters upon request.

## Charging During Temple Visits

Temple visits, especially during festivals or peak times, can be lengthy. Ensure your devices are fully charged before visiting temples, as charging facilities may not be available inside temple premises. Power banks are particularly useful during these visits. When visiting Ram Mandir, plan your [darshan timing](/blog/ayodhya-early-morning-darshan-tips-ram-mandir) and ensure devices are charged accordingly.

## WiFi and Internet Connectivity

Many accommodations provide WiFi internet connectivity for guests. If staying at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, check WiFi availability and connectivity details when booking. WiFi allows you to stay connected, share photos, and access online resources during your visit. Verify internet facilities through the [official contact number](/contact-number) to ensure connectivity meets your needs.

## Energy Conservation Tips

Be mindful of energy conservation during your stay. Unplug chargers when devices are fully charged to save energy. Use devices efficiently to extend battery life, especially during temple visits. Plan device usage to minimize the need for frequent charging. These practices help conserve energy and ensure facilities remain available for all guests.
