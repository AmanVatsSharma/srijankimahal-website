# Ayodhya Emergency Contacts and Helpline Numbers

Having access to emergency contacts and helpline numbers is essential for a safe and secure pilgrimage to Ayodhya. Whether you need medical assistance, police help, fire services, or tourist support, knowing the right numbers to call can make a significant difference during an emergency. This guide provides essential emergency contacts and helpline numbers for your Ayodhya visit.

## Police Emergency Services

For any law enforcement needs, security concerns, or reporting incidents, contact the local police. The Ayodhya Police Station handles security matters, lost and found items, and general assistance for pilgrims. Keep the police emergency number saved in your phone for quick access. If you encounter any suspicious activity or need immediate security assistance, don't hesitate to contact the police helpline.

## Medical Emergency Services

Medical emergencies require immediate attention. Ayodhya has hospitals and medical facilities that provide emergency services. For serious medical emergencies, call the ambulance service immediately. If you're staying at accommodation like Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, check the [location guide](/location) to identify the nearest medical facilities. The trust's proximity to Ram Mandir, detailed in the [location guide near Ram Mandir](/location/near-ram-mandir), ensures access to medical services. For non-emergency medical needs, pharmacies and clinics are available throughout the city.

## Fire Emergency Services

In case of fire emergencies, contact the fire department immediately. Fire safety is important, especially in crowded areas during festivals. If you notice any fire hazards or need fire emergency assistance, use the fire helpline number. Accommodations like Sri Janaki Mahal Trust maintain fire safety standards, as detailed in their [official information](/official). Always follow fire safety guidelines and report any concerns promptly.

## Tourist Helpline and Assistance

The tourist helpline provides assistance with travel-related queries, lost belongings, directions, and general information about Ayodhya. Tourist assistance centers help pilgrims navigate the city, find accommodations, and access temple information. If you need help with [booking verification](/booking-verification-guide) or have questions about your stay, contact the tourist helpline or reach out through the [official contact number](/contact-number).

For transportation-related emergencies or assistance, contact local transport authorities or your accommodation.
