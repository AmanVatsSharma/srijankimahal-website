# Ayodhya in January - Weather and Janaki Mahal Stay

January is a pleasant time for Ayodhya—cool weather and Makar Sankranti. Here's how to plan and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Weather

- **Temperature**: Cool, 5–20°C. Carry warm clothes
- **Makar Sankranti**: Mid-January—Saryu snan; book early

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). For Sankranti, see [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide) and [Makar Sankranti guide](/blog/janaki-mahal-trust-makar-sankranti-bathing-festival).
