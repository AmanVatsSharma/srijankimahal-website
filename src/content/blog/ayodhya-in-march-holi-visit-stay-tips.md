# Ayodhya in March - Holi Visit and Stay Tips

March brings Holi and warmer weather to Ayodhya. Here's how to plan and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Weather

- **Temperature**: 15–32°C. Light cotton clothes
- **Holi**: Check dates; may affect crowds
