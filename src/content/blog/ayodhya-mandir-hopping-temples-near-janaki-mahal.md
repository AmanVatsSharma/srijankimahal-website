# Ayodhya Mandir Hopping - Temples Near Janaki Mahal Trust

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is a convenient base for visiting multiple temples in Ayodhya. Here's a suggested temple circuit.

## Nearby Temples

- **Ram Mandir**: Walking distance—main darshan
- **Hanuman Garhi**: Important temple; short ride or walk
- **Kanak Bhawan**: Dedicated to Sita-Ram; near Ram Mandir
- **Nageshwarnath Temple**: One of the ancient temples
- **Saryu Ghat**: For holy bath and evening aarti—see [Saryu aarti guide](/blog/ayodhya-saryu-aarti-guide-timings-etiquette)

## Suggested Itinerary

**Day 1**: Ram Mandir, Kanak Bhawan, Hanuman Garhi  
**Day 2**: Nageshwarnath, Saryu Ghat, other temples

See [2-day itinerary](/blog/ayodhya-2-day-itinerary-ram-mandir-stay) and [3-day itinerary](/blog/ayodhya-3-day-itinerary-temples-ghats-food) for detailed plans.

## From Janaki Mahal

The trust is in Karsewakpuram. Auto or e-rickshaw can take you to temples beyond walking distance. See [location](/location) and [local transport tips](/blog/ayodhya-local-transport-auto-e-rickshaw-taxi-tips).
