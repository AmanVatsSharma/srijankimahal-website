# Ayodhya Meti Ghat to Janaki Mahal - Walking Route & Distance

Meti Ghat is a significant spot on the Saryu. If you're staying at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust, here's how to plan the walk between Meti Ghat and the trust.

## Distance and Time

- **Approximate distance**: 2–3 km (depending on exact route)
- **Walking time**: 25–40 minutes at a moderate pace
- **Terrain**: Mostly flat; some narrow lanes near the temple area

## Route Overview

From Meti Ghat, head toward Ram Mandir / Karsewakpuram. Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is in Karsewakpuram. You can ask locals for "Janaki Mahal Trust" or "Karsewakpuram"; most know the area.

## Tips for the Walk

- **Footwear**: Comfortable shoes—paths may be uneven in places
- **Water**: Carry a bottle, especially in summer
- **Mornings**: Cooler and less crowded for walking
- **Directions**: Use the [location guide](/location) and [official address](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-karsewakpuram-address) for reference

## After Saryu Aarti

If you attend Saryu aarti and then walk back, the route to Janaki Mahal Trust is straightforward. See [Saryu aarti guide](/blog/ayodhya-saryu-aarti-guide-timings-etiquette) for timings.
