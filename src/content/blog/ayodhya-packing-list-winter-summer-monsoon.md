# Ayodhya Packing List (Winter, Summer, Monsoon) — What to Carry

Packing light but smart makes darshan days easier—especially if you plan walking time and queues.

## Winter (Oct–Mar)

- Light warm layer + socks
- Comfortable walking shoes

## Summer (Apr–Jun)

- Water bottle + ORS
- Cap + light cotton clothing

## Monsoon (Jul–Sep)

- Compact raincoat/umbrella
- Extra footwear care (wet streets)
