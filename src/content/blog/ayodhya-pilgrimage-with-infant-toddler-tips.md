# Ayodhya Pilgrimage With Infant or Toddler - Tips

Families with infants/toddlers can stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust. Here are some tips.

## Stay

- **Rooms**: Family rooms, 2-bed with space. See [rooms](/rooms) and [family rooms](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-family-rooms)
- **Meals**: Included; inform staff of baby food needs when [booking](/official-booking)
- **Safety**: Supervise children. See [family with kids](/blog/ayodhya-family-trip-with-kids-stay-darshan-tips)

## Darshan

Plan short visits. Avoid peak crowd times. Carry essentials.

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number).
