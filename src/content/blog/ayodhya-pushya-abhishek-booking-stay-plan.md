# Ayodhya Pushya Abhishek - Booking & Stay Plan at Janaki Mahal

Pushya Abhishek is a special day at Ram Mandir. Planning your stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust helps you participate smoothly.

## Pushya Abhishek Significance

Pushya Nakshatra is considered highly auspicious. Special abhishek and darshan are performed at Ram Mandir. Check the Hindu calendar for Pushya dates each month.
