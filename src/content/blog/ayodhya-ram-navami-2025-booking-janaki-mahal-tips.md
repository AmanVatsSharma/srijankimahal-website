# Ayodhya Ram Navami 2025 Booking - Janaki Mahal Trust Tips

Ram Navami draws huge crowds to Ayodhya. Securing a room at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust requires early planning. Here's how.

## When to Book

**Book at least 2–3 months in advance.** Rooms fill quickly for Ram Navami. Contact the [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number) as soon as your dates are fixed.

## Ram Navami Dates

Ram Navami falls in Chaitra month (March–April). Check the Hindu calendar for the exact date each year. Plan your stay to cover the main day and possibly a day before and after.

## What to Expect

- **Crowds**: Very high—arrive early for darshan, plan extra time for transport
- **Rates**: May be at peak; confirm [room prices](/rooms) when booking
- **Meals**: Trust provides meals—see [meals and food guide](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-meals-food-guide)

## Avoid Scams

Only book through [official channels](/official-booking). Scammers target festival periods. Follow [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams) and verify every confirmation via the [booking verification guide](/booking-verification-guide).
