# Ayodhya Festival Crowd Tips (Ram Navami, Diwali) + Booking Strategy

Festivals bring heavy footfall. The best strategy is **early planning + verified booking**.

## Practical tips

- Book early and keep confirmation saved.
- Prefer morning darshan to reduce heat/crowd stress.
- Keep extra buffer for security checks and transport.
