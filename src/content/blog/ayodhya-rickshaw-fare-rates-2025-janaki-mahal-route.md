# Ayodhya Rickshaw Fare Rates 2025 - Janaki Mahal Trust Route Guide

Knowing approximate rickshaw and auto fares helps you plan transport and avoid overcharging in Ayodhya.

## Ayodhya Junction to Sri Janaki Mahal Trust (Karsewakpuram)

- **Auto/rickshaw**: Approximately ₹50–100 (negotiate before boarding)
- **E-rickshaw**: ₹30–80 depending on luggage and number of passengers
- **Distance**: About 3–4 km

Rates may be higher during festivals and late night.

## General Fare Tips

1. **Negotiate upfront**: Agree on fare before the ride.
2. **Shared vs private**: Shared rickshaws cost less; private costs more.
3. **Peak periods**: Expect 20–30% higher during festivals.
4. **Night travel**: Slightly higher; ensure you have the [official contact](/contact-number) for Sri Janaki Mahal Trust in case of issues.

## From Other Points

- **Bus stand to Karsewakpuram**: ₹40–80
- **Airport (if you fly to Ayodhya)**: See [location from airport](/location/from-airport) for pickup options

## Sri Janaki Mahal Trust Location

The trust is in Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir. Tell drivers "Janaki Mahal Trust, Karsewakpuram" or "near Ram Mandir." For directions, check the [location guide](/location).
