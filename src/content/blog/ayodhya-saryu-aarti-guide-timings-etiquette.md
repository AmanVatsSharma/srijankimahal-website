# Saryu Aarti in Ayodhya — Simple Guide (Timing + Etiquette + Planning)

Saryu ghat time is often a highlight of an Ayodhya trip. Treat it as a calm, unhurried block in your itinerary.

## How to plan it with darshan

- Do Ram Mandir darshan in the morning.
- Keep Saryu aarti as an evening plan (with buffer).

## Basic etiquette

- Keep the area clean and avoid crowd pushing.
- Respect local instructions and the sanctity of the ghat.
