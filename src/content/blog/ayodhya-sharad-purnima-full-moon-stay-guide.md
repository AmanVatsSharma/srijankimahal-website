# Ayodhya Sharad Purnima Full Moon - Stay Guide & Janaki Mahal Booking

Sharad Purnima (Kojagari Purnima) falls in Ashwin month. Ayodhya sees increased pilgrim activity. Here's how to plan your stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Sharad Purnima Significance

The full moon of Sharad is considered auspicious. Devotees visit temples, including Ram Mandir, and observe the moon. The weather is usually pleasant—a good time for pilgrimage.

## Booking Your Stay

- **Book 2–4 weeks ahead**
- Contact [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number)
- Check [room types](/rooms)

## What to Expect

- Moderate to high crowds depending on the day
- Pleasant evening weather
- Special darshan or programs may be held—check temple announcements
