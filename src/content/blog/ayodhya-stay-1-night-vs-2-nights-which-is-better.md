# Ayodhya Stay: 1 Night vs 2 Nights — Which Is Better for Darshan?

## Choose 1 night if

- You want one darshan session and quick return.
- You are okay with a tighter schedule.

## Choose 2 nights if

- You want **morning + evening** darshan.
- You have elders/kids and need rest buffers.
