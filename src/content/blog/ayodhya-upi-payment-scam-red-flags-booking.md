# Ayodhya Booking Payment Scam Red Flags (UPI Links) — What to Avoid

If someone asks you to pay via an unknown link or personal account, pause and verify first.

## Common red flags

- Payment request before a clear written confirmation
- Different number than the official contact
- “Pay now or you lose the room” pressure tactics
- Payment to personal accounts or suspicious QR/links

## What to do instead

1) Verify official channels:
- `/official-booking`
- `/contact-number`
