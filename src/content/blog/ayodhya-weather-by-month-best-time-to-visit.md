# Ayodhya Weather by Month + Best Time to Visit (Quick Guide)

Ayodhya travel comfort depends heavily on season. This quick guide helps you choose dates and plan booking early for peak days.

## Practical season buckets

- **Oct–Mar**: generally more comfortable for walking and long darshan time
- **Apr–Jun**: hotter months (plan morning darshan + rest breaks)
- **Jul–Sep**: monsoon months (carry rain protection, plan buffers)
