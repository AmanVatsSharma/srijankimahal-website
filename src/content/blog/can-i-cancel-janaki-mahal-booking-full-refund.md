# Can I Cancel Janaki Mahal Booking - Full Refund Policy

Yes, you can cancel. Refund depends on cancellation timing. See the [cancellation and refund policy](/cancellation-refund) for full details.

## General Policy

- **Early cancellation**: Full or partial refund based on how far in advance you cancel
- **Late cancellation**: May have reduced or no refund
- **Festival bookings**: Stricter terms often apply

Contact the [official number](/contact-number) to request cancellation and refund.

## How to Cancel

1. Contact [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number)
2. Provide your booking ID and dates
3. Request cancellation and refund as per policy
4. Get confirmation in writing

Never cancel through third-party agents. See [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams). For detailed terms, visit [cancellation-refund](/cancellation-refund).
