# Can I Extend My Stay at Janaki Mahal Trust?

Yes, you can extend if rooms are available. Contact the trust as early as possible.

## How to Extend

1. **Contact**: Call or WhatsApp [official number](/contact-number) or speak to reception
2. **Check availability**: Staff will confirm if your room or another is free
3. **Payment**: Pay for extra nights as per [room rates](/rooms)
4. **Confirmation**: Get updated confirmation in writing
