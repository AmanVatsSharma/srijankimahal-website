# Chennai to Ayodhya Train & Bus - Janaki Mahal Stay Guide

Chennai to Ayodhya is a long but popular pilgrimage route. Here's how to plan and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Chennai

**Stations**: Chennai Central (MAS) / Egmore to Ayodhya Junction  
**Duration**: Approximately 32–38 hours  
**Route**: Via Vijayawada, Nagpur, or alternative

Book early on IRCTC. Festival dates fill quickly—see [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Bus from Chennai

Long-distance buses available. Journey 36–40 hours. Book ahead for festivals.

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal

Trust is in Karsewakpuram. See [location guide](/location).

## Book Your Stay

Reserve via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). Check [rooms](/rooms).
