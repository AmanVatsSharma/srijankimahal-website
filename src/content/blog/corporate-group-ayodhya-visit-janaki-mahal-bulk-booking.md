# Corporate Group Ayodhya Visit - Janaki Mahal Bulk Booking

Corporate groups can book Sri Janaki Mahal Trust in bulk. Contact early for multiple rooms.

## Process

1. **Contact**: [official number](/contact-number) or [official booking](/official-booking)
2. **Share**: Dates, number of guests, room preference (AC/non-AC)
3. **Confirm**: Get written confirmation. See [booking verification guide](/booking-verification-guide)
4. **Payment**: Through official trust account only. See [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams)

## Room Options

See [room types](/rooms) and [group booking](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-group-booking). Multi-bed and family rooms available.
