# Delhi to Ayodhya Overnight Train - Booking Tips & Janaki Mahal Stay

Overnight trains from Delhi to Ayodhya are a convenient way to reach the city. Here’s how to plan your journey and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Popular Overnight Trains

Several trains connect New Delhi / Delhi Junction with Ayodhya Junction (AY):
- **Duration**: Approximately 10–14 hours
- **Classes**: Sleeper, AC 3-tier, AC 2-tier
- **Check IRCTC** for current schedules and availability

## Booking Tips

1. **Book early**: Especially for weekends and festivals—see [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide)
2. **Return tickets**: Book return trains in advance if your dates are fixed
3. **PNR and chart**: Check charting status before travel
4. **Arrival time**: Plan check-in at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust (typically from 12 noon)—[check-in timings](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-check-in-check-out-timings)

## From Ayodhya Junction to Janaki Mahal

- Auto/rickshaw: About 15–20 minutes
- The trust is in Karsewakpuram—see [location from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction)

## Booking Your Stay

Reserve your room in advance via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). If you arrive early morning, confirm with the trust about early check-in or luggage storage.
