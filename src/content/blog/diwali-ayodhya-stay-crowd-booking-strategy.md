# Diwali Ayodhya Stay - Crowd & Booking Strategy for Janaki Mahal

Diwali in Ayodhya is a peak period. Here's how to plan your stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust and manage the crowds.

## Booking Strategy

- **Book 2–3 months in advance**—rooms sell out quickly
- Use only [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number)
- Confirm your dates—Diwali spans several days; decide if you want the main day or a day before/after

## Crowd Expectations

- **Very high footfall** around Diwali
- Transport and darshan queues can be long
- Plan extra time for travel and temple visit
- Stay hydrated and follow [safety tips](/blog/ayodhya-night-stay-safety-security-tips)

## At Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

- Meals are included—see [meals guide](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-meals-food-guide)
- Location near Ram Mandir—see [location](/location)
- Rooms: check [room types](/rooms) and book early

## Scam Alert

Festival periods attract fraud. Never pay via unverified channels. Follow [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams) and [booking verification guide](/booking-verification-guide).
