# Does Janaki Mahal Accept Credit Card Payment?

Payment methods at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust may include cash, UPI, and bank transfer. Credit card acceptance—confirm with [official contact](/contact-number).

## Payment Options

- **Cash**: Generally accepted at reception
- **UPI**: Often accepted. See [cash vs UPI guide](/blog/janaki-mahal-trust-cash-vs-upi-payment-preference)
- **Bank transfer**: For advance payment—beneficiary must be Sri Janaki Mahal Trust
- **Credit/Debit card**: Confirm with trust when you [book](/official-booking)

## Safe Payment

Pay only through official channels. Never use personal UPI IDs. See [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams).

## Book Your Stay

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms).
