# Does Janaki Mahal Provide Pickup From Station?

Pickup from Ayodhya Junction may or may not be offered. Confirm with [official contact](/contact-number) when you [book](/official-booking).

## Alternatives

- **Auto/rickshaw**: Available at station. Approximately ₹50–100 to Karsewakpuram. See [rickshaw fare guide](/blog/ayodhya-rickshaw-fare-rates-2025-janaki-mahal-route)
- **E-rickshaw**: Cost-effective option
- **Walking**: Possible with light luggage; see [location from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction)
