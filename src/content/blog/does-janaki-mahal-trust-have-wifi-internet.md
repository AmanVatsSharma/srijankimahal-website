# Does Janaki Mahal Trust Have WiFi Internet?

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust provides WiFi/internet for guests. See [WiFi guide](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-wifi-internet) for details.

## What to Expect

- **WiFi**: Generally available in common areas and sometimes in rooms
- **Speed**: Suitable for basic browsing and messaging
- **Backup**: Mobile data works in Ayodhya; keep a local SIM or roaming

Confirm current facility with [official contact](/contact-number) when you [book](/official-booking).

## For Work or Video

If you need strong connectivity for work or video calls, check with the trust. Mobile hotspot can supplement.

## Book Your Stay

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms).
