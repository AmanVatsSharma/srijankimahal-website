# Gorakhpur to Ayodhya Route - Janaki Mahal Stay Guide

Gorakhpur to Ayodhya is a short and convenient route. Here's how to travel and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Gorakhpur

**Stations**: Gorakhpur Junction (GKP) to Ayodhya Junction (AY)  
**Duration**: Approximately 2–4 hours  
**Frequency**: Multiple trains

## Bus from Gorakhpur

UPSRTC buses. Journey 3–5 hours.
