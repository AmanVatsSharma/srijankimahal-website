# Hyderabad to Ayodhya Pilgrimage Route - Janaki Mahal Stay

Hyderabad to Ayodhya pilgrimage is well-connected. Here's how to travel and book Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Hyderabad

**Stations**: Hyderabad (HYB) / Secunderabad to Ayodhya Junction  
**Duration**: Approximately 24–28 hours  
**Route**: Via Nagpur, Jabalpur, or alternative routes

Book on IRCTC. For festivals, see [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Flight from Hyderabad

**Airports**: Hyderabad (HYD) to Lucknow or Ayodhya  
Then train/bus to Ayodhya. See [Lucknow to Ayodhya](/blog/lucknow-to-ayodhya-travel-options-airport-train-taxi).

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal

Trust is in Karsewakpuram. Auto 15–20 min from station. See [location](/location).

## Book Your Stay

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms).
