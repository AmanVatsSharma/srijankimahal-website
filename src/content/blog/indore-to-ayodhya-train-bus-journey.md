# Indore to Ayodhya Train & Bus Journey

Indore to Ayodhya pilgrimage options include train and bus. Here's how to plan and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Indore

**Stations**: Indore (INDB) to Ayodhya Junction  
**Duration**: Approximately 20–26 hours  
**Route**: Via Ujjain, Bhopal, or alternative

Book on IRCTC. See [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Bus from Indore

Long-distance buses. Journey 22–28 hours.

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal

Trust is in Karsewakpuram. See [location](/location).

## Book Your Stay

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms).
