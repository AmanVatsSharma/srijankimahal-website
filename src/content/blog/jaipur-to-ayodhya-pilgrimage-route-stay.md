# Jaipur to Ayodhya Pilgrimage Route - Janaki Mahal Stay

Jaipur to Ayodhya pilgrimage options include train and bus. Here's how to plan and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Jaipur

**Stations**: Jaipur Junction (JP) to Ayodhya Junction  
**Duration**: Approximately 12–16 hours  
**Route**: Via Delhi or Agra

Book on IRCTC. See [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Bus from Jaipur

Long-distance buses. Journey 14–18 hours.

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal

Trust is in Karsewakpuram. See [location](/location).

## Book Your Stay

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms). Avoid [scams](/how-to-avoid-scams).
