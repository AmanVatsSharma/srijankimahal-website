# Janaki Mahal Booking Not Confirmed - What to Do

If you haven't received confirmation from Sri Janaki Mahal Trust:

1. **Wait**: Peak season can delay response. See [booking wait time](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-booking-wait-time-peak-season)
2. **Follow up**: Call or WhatsApp [official number](/contact-number) +91 9893563047
3. **Verify channel**: Ensure you contacted only the official number. See [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams)
4. **Check format**: See [booking verification guide](/booking-verification-guide) for correct confirmation format

Do not pay until you receive proper confirmation. Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number).
