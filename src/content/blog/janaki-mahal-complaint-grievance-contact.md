# Janaki Mahal Complaint and Grievance - Contact

To lodge a complaint or grievance about Sri Janaki Mahal Trust:

1. **Reception**: Speak to staff on-site first
2. **Contact**: [official number](/contact-number) +91 7759925612 or email srijanakimahaltrustofficial@gmail.com
3. **Details**: Provide booking ID, dates, and clear description
4. **Follow-up**: Request reference number or acknowledgment

See [official verification](/official) and [contact number](/contact-number). For general feedback, see [post-visit guide](/blog/ayodhya-post-visit-follow-up-stay-connected-feedback-guide).
