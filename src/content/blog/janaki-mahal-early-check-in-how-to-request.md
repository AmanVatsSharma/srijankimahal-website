# Janaki Mahal Early Check-In - How to Request

To request early check-in at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust:

1. **Inform in advance**: Mention when [booking](/official-booking) or contact [official number](/contact-number)
2. **Subject to availability**: Rooms may not be ready before standard check-in (usually 12 noon)
3. **Luggage**: Staff may hold luggage if room not ready. See [luggage storage](/blog/ayodhya-luggage-storage-lockers-near-ram-mandir)
