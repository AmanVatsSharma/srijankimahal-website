# Janaki Mahal Lost and Found - Luggage Policy

If you lose items at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust:

1. **Inform immediately**: Tell reception or contact [official number](/contact-number)
2. **Description**: Provide detailed description of item
3. **Contact**: Leave your contact for callback if found
4. **Luggage**: See [luggage security](/blog/ayodhya-stay-luggage-security-safe-keeping) and [luggage storage](/blog/ayodhya-luggage-storage-lockers-near-ram-mandir)

Lock your room and keep valuables secure. See [rooms](/rooms). Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number).
