# Janaki Mahal Trust Cash vs UPI Payment - What to Prefer

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust accepts payments as per their policy. Here's what to expect and how to pay safely.

## Payment Methods

- **Cash**: Accepted at reception
- **UPI**: May be accepted—confirm when you receive [booking confirmation](/booking-verification-guide)
- **Bank transfer**: For advance payment—beneficiary must be **Sri Janaki Mahal Trust**

**Confirm current methods** with the [official contact](/contact-number) when you book.

## When to Use Cash

- **On arrival**: If UPI or card fails
- **Small amounts**: For tips, donations, or local purchases
- **Backup**: ATMs available—see [ATM guide](/blog/ayodhya-currency-exchange-atm-near-ram-mandir)

## When to Use UPI

- **Convenient**: No need to carry large cash
- **Record**: Digital trail for your payment
- **Safety**: Avoid [UPI scams](/blog/ayodhya-upi-payment-scam-red-flags-booking)—pay only to official trust accounts

## Safe Payment Rules

- Pay only to accounts named **Sri Janaki Mahal Trust** or **Janaki Mahal Trust Ayodhya**
- Never pay to personal UPI IDs
- Verify via [official verification](/official) and [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams)
