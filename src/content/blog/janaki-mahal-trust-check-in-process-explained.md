# Janaki Mahal Trust Check-in Process Explained - Official Guidelines

Arriving at **Janaki Mahal Trust** (also referred to as **Sri Janki Mahal Trust** and **Shri Janki Mahal Trust**) is smooth when you follow the verified check-in process. This guide walks you through every step to ensure a quick and peaceful start to your stay.

## Standard Check-in & Check-out Timings

- **Check-in:** 2:00 PM onwards
- **Check-out:** 12:00 PM (noon)
- **Early Check-in / Late Check-out:** Subject to room availability; inform the trust in advance.

## Documents to Carry

1. Government-issued photo ID for every guest (Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID).
2. Printed or digital **trust-stamped confirmation slip**.
3. Payment receipt or transaction confirmation (if paid in advance).
4. For group bookings: List of guest names and phone numbers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I check in early if my train arrives in the morning?
Yes, subject to room availability. If rooms are being prepared, you can rest in the common lounge and enjoy breakfast.

### What if I misplaced my confirmation slip?
Contact the official helpline. The team resends the slip via WhatsApp or email instantly.

### Are visitors allowed in rooms?
Visitors are allowed between 9 AM and 9 PM after entering the visitor register. Overnight stays are reserved for registered guests only.

### Can I request adjoining rooms?
Yes. Mention it during booking or at least 48 hours before arrival so arrangements can be made.
