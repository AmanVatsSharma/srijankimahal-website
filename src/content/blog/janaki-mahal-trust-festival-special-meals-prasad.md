# Janaki Mahal Trust Festival Special Meals & Prasad (What to Expect)

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust provides meals as part of your stay. During festivals, the menu may include special items. Here's what to expect.

## Regular Meals

The trust offers breakfast, lunch, and dinner as part of the room tariff. Meals are vegetarian and suitable for pilgrims. See [meals and food guide](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-meals-food-guide).

## Festival Specials

During major festivals (Ram Navami, Diwali, Makar Sankranti, etc.):
- **Special dishes**: May include festival-specific items (e.g., kheer, puri, seasonal sweets)
- **Prasad-style**: Food is prepared with devotion and served in a sattvic manner
- **Timing**: Meal timings may be adjusted for festival schedules—confirm with staff

**Exact menu** varies—ask at check-in or contact the [official number](/contact-number).

## Dietary Needs

If you have allergies or dietary restrictions, inform the trust when you [book](/official-booking). They will try to accommodate within their capacity.

## Booking for Festivals

Festival stays require early booking. Use [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact](/contact-number). See [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).
