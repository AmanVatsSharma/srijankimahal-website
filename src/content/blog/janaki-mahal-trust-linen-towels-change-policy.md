# Janaki Mahal Trust Linen & Towels Change Policy

Clean linens and towels contribute to a comfortable stay. Here's what to expect at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Standard Policy

- **Check-in**: Fresh bed sheets, pillow covers, and towels are provided when you arrive
- **During stay**: Linens may be changed periodically based on length of stay—typically every 2–3 days or on request
- **Towels**: Same as linens; request a change at reception if needed

**Confirm current policy** with staff at check-in or via the [official contact](/contact-number).

## For Long Stays

If you're staying a week or more, the trust will arrange linen changes. Ask about the schedule when you [book](/official-booking).

## Personal Preference

- **Own towel**: You may bring your own towel if preferred
- **Sensitive skin**: Use your own toiletries if you have allergies
- **Request**: Don’t hesitate to ask for extra or changed linen—staff aim to accommodate

## Hygiene

The trust maintains clean standards. Linens are washed and sanitized. For additional hygiene tips during pilgrimage, see [water hygiene](/blog/ayodhya-water-hygiene-tips-pilgrims).
