# Janaki Mahal Trust - Makar Sankranti Bathing Festival Stay Guide

Makar Sankranti brings devotees to Ayodhya for the holy Saryu bath. Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is a convenient base for this festival.

## Makar Sankranti in Ayodhya

- **Date**: Usually January 14–15 (solar transition)
- **Activity**: Holy dip in Saryu at dawn
- **Crowds**: High—book early

## Booking Your Stay

- **Book 4–6 weeks in advance**
- Contact [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number)
- Rooms fill quickly; see [room types](/rooms) and [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide)

## From Janaki Mahal to Saryu Ghat

The trust is in Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir and Saryu. Ghats are within reach by walk or short ride. See [location guide](/location) and [Saryu aarti guide](/blog/ayodhya-saryu-aarti-guide-timings-etiquette).

## Post-Bath

After the bath, return to the trust for a warm change and meals. Meals are included—see [meals guide](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-meals-food-guide).

## Book Safely

Use only [official channels](/official-booking). Follow [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams).
