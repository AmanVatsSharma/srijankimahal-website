# Janaki Mahal Trust Payment Options & Advance Booking Guide

Securing your stay at **Janaki Mahal Trust** (also called **Sri Janki Mahal Trust** and **Shri Janki Mahal Trust**) requires using the official payment channels. This guide outlines trusted methods, advance booking timelines, and safety tips.

> Flow Chart: Confirm room availability ‚Üí Receive trust-stamped slip ‚Üí Pay through official channel ‚Üí Share transaction proof ‚Üí Receive final confirmation.

## Accepted Payment Methods

1. **UPI Transfers:** Official UPI ID shared via verified helpline.
2. **Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS):** Trust bank account details provided after confirmation.
3. **Credit/Debit Cards:** Accepted at reception for on-site payments (select terminals).
4. **Cash:** Pay at reception for walk-ins or balance amounts.

> Comment: The trust never asks for payment through personal accounts or unknown links. Always double-check with **+91 9102319329**.

## Advance Booking Policy

- **Standard Dates:** Pay a token advance (20-30%) within 24 hours of confirmation.
- **Festival Seasons:** Full payment may be required to lock rooms.
- **Group Bookings:** 30-50% advance recommended to reserve multiple rooms.
- **Modification Requests:** Subject to availability; call the trust as early as possible.

## Step-by-Step Payment Flow

1. Contact the official helpline or WhatsApp.
2. Receive the trust-stamped confirmation slip with booking ID.
3. Choose your preferred payment method (UPI/Bank Transfer).
4. Complete payment and save the transaction reference.
5. Share payment proof on the same WhatsApp thread or email.
6. Receive payment acknowledgement and final check-in instructions.

## Safety Tips

- Only trust details shared from **+91 9102319329** or [srijanakimahaltrustofficial@gmail.com](mailto:srijanakimahaltrustofficial@gmail.com).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I pay the entire amount at check-in?
Yes for non-festival days, but advance payment is recommended to avoid last-minute unavailability.

### Do you provide GST invoices?
Yes. Share your GST details during booking or at check-in to receive an invoice.

### Is there an online payment link?
Authorized links are shared on WhatsApp/email directly from the trust. Never click on unofficial links.

### What happens if my payment fails?
Contact the helpline immediately. The trust verifies the status and provides alternate options.
