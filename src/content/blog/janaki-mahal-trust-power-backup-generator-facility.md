# Janaki Mahal Trust Power Backup & Generator Facility

Power cuts can occur in Ayodhya. Here's what to expect regarding backup power at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Backup Power

The trust has power backup arrangements to support essential operations during short power cuts. This helps maintain lights, fans, and basic facilities when the main supply is interrupted.

## What Is Typically Covered

- Common area lighting
- Essential room power (fans, lights)
- Reception and security systems
