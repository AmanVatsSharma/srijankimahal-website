# Janaki Mahal Trust Reachable by Two-Wheeler - Parking & Access Guide

Traveling to Ayodhya on a bike or scooter? Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is accessible by two-wheeler. Here's what you need to know about parking and access.

## Road Access

The trust is located in Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir. Roads leading to the area accommodate two-wheelers. During festivals, traffic may be dense—plan for extra time.

## Parking at or Near the Trust

- **On-site parking**: The trust has [parking facilities](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-parking-facilities) for guests. Two-wheelers can usually be accommodated.
- **Nearby parking**: If on-site space is full during peak periods, designated parking areas may be available nearby—ask staff on arrival.

Contact the [official number](/contact-number) to confirm current parking availability.

## Security

- Park in designated areas
- Lock your vehicle
- Keep documents (RC, licence) with you
- Don’t leave valuables in the vehicle

## From Ayodhya Junction

If you bring a two-wheeler by train (as luggage), collect it and ride to Karsewakpuram. See [location guide](/location) and [from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction) for directions.
