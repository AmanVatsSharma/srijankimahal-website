# Janki Mahal Trust Near Ram Mandir - Distance & Directions Guide

- **Walking Distance:** 2.3 km (approx. 25-30 minutes at a gentle pace)
- **Google Maps Pin:** Search “Sri Janaki Mahal Trust” or “Janki Mahal Trust Ayodhya”

## Recommended Travel Routes

### By Auto / Taxi

1. Exit Ram Mandir complex from the main gate.
2. Hire an auto and mention “Janaki Mahal Trust near Vasudev Ghat.”
3. Follow the Ram Path towards Karsewakpuram.
4. Continue straight until you spot the Sri Janaki Mahal Trust signage.

### By Foot

1. Start from Ram Mandir main entrance.
2. Walk along the well-lit Ram Path.
3. Turn right towards Karsewakpuram Chowk.
4. Follow signboards leading to **Sri Janaki Mahal Trust**.

> Comment: Walking is recommended in early mornings or evenings to avoid midday heat.
