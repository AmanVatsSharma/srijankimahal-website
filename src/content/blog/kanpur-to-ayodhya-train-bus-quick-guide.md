# Kanpur to Ayodhya Train & Bus - Quick Guide

Kanpur is close to Ayodhya. Here's the quick route and how to book Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Kanpur

**Stations**: Kanpur Central (CNB) to Ayodhya Junction (AY)  
**Duration**: Approximately 3–5 hours  
**Frequency**: Multiple trains daily

## Bus from Kanpur

UPSRTC and private buses. Journey 4–6 hours.

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal

Trust is in Karsewakpuram. Auto 15–20 min. See [location](/location).
