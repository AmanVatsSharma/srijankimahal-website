# Kashi Ayodhya Pilgrimage Package - Janaki Mahal Stay

Combine Kashi (Varanasi) and Ayodhya. Book Sri Janaki Mahal Trust for the Ayodhya segment. See [Varanasi to Ayodhya](/blog/varanasi-to-ayodhya-train-bus-route-janaki-mahal).

## Itinerary

- **Kashi**: Sangam, temples
- **Travel**: Train or bus to Ayodhya
- **Ayodhya**: Stay at Janaki Mahal, Ram Mandir darshan
