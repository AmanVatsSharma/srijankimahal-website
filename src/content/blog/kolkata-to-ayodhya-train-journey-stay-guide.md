# Kolkata to Ayodhya Train Journey - Janaki Mahal Stay Guide

Kolkata to Ayodhya by train is a popular pilgrimage route. Here's how to plan your journey and stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train Options

**Stations**: Howrah (HWH) / Sealdah to Ayodhya Junction (AY)  
**Duration**: Approximately 16–22 hours  
**Route**: Via Gaya, Varanasi, or Prayagraj

Check IRCTC for schedules. Book early for festivals—see [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Suggested Trains

Several express trains connect Kolkata and Ayodhya. Overnight trains let you reach Ayodhya by morning.

## From Ayodhya Junction

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is in Karsewakpuram. Auto/rickshaw: 15–20 min. See [location from Ayodhya Junction](/location/from-ayodhya-junction).

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). Check [rooms](/rooms).
