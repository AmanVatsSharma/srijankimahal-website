# Mumbai to Ayodhya Train, Bus & Flight - Janaki Mahal Stay Guide

Planning your Ayodhya pilgrimage from Mumbai? This guide covers train, bus, and flight options and how to reach Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Mumbai

**Stations**: Mumbai Central / Dadar / LTT to Ayodhya Junction (AY)  
**Duration**: Approximately 24–30 hours  
**Frequency**: Multiple trains via Prayagraj, Varanasi

Book early via IRCTC. For festival dates, see the [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Flight from Mumbai

**Airports**: Mumbai (BOM) to Ayodhya Airport or Lucknow  
**Ayodhya Airport**: Direct flights available; then short drive to Karsewakpuram  
**Lucknow**: Fly to Lucknow, then train/bus to Ayodhya (see [Lucknow to Ayodhya](/blog/lucknow-to-ayodhya-travel-options-airport-train-taxi))

## Bus from Mumbai

Long-distance buses run Mumbai–Ayodhya. Journey 24–30 hours. Book through UPSRTC or private operators.

## From Ayodhya to Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is in Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir. From station or airport: auto 15–20 min. See [location guide](/location).

## Book Your Stay

Reserve in advance via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). Check [rooms](/rooms) for options.
