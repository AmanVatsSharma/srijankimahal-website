# Nagpur to Ayodhya Pilgrimage Route - Janaki Mahal Stay

Nagpur to Ayodhya is well-connected. Here's how to travel and book Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train from Nagpur

**Stations**: Nagpur (NGP) to Ayodhya Junction  
**Duration**: Approximately 16–22 hours  
**Route**: Via Jabalpur, Prayagraj

Book on IRCTC. See [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide).

## Bus from Nagpur

Long-distance buses. Journey 18–24 hours.

## From Ayodhya to Janaki Mahal
