# Night Travel to Ayodhya - Safety Tips & Late Arrival Checklist

Many pilgrims reach Ayodhya by overnight train or bus. Here's how to stay safe and manage a late-night arrival at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Pre-Arrival Checklist

1. **Confirm booking**: Ensure you have a verified [booking confirmation](/booking-verification-guide) from the [official contact](/contact-number).
2. **Share arrival details**: Inform the trust of your expected arrival time so they can accommodate you.
3. **Save contact**: Keep +91 7759925612 saved as "Janaki Mahal Trust Official."
4. **Know the address**: Karsewakpuram, near Ram Mandir—see [location guide](/location).

## At the Station or Bus Stand

- Use pre-paid auto/rickshaw when available
- Avoid unverified "hotel agents" offering rooms
- Stick to well-lit areas; travel in groups when possible

## Transport at Night

- Auto and e-rickshaw may be less frequent but usually available
- Negotiate fare before boarding
- Share your live location with family if possible

## Check-In at Janaki Mahal Trust

The trust may have limited staff at very late hours. Confirm with [official contact](/contact-number) if you are arriving after 10–11 PM. They can advise on reception timings and late check-in procedure.

## General Safety

- Keep valuables secure
- Avoid sharing booking or payment details with strangers
- Book only through [official channels](/official-booking)—see [how to avoid scams](/how-to-avoid-scams)
