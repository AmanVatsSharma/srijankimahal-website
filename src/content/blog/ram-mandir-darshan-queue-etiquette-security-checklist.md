# Ram Mandir Darshan Queue Etiquette + Security Checklist (Ayodhya)

Darshan becomes smoother when you plan for security checks and keep your belongings minimal.

## Security-ready checklist

- Carry minimal items (avoid large bags)
- Keep your phone on silent mode
- Follow posted instructions at entry points

## Queue etiquette (simple)

- Stay calm, keep space for elders/kids
- Avoid pushing or rushing
- Plan a buffer for wait time
