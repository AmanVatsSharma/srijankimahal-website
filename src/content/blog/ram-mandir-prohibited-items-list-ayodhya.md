# Ram Mandir Prohibited Items List - Ayodhya

Ram Mandir has restrictions on what you can carry. Check current rules at the temple or official site.

## Commonly Prohibited

- **Phones/cameras**: May be restricted. See [photography rules](/blog/ram-mandir-photography-rules-mobile-phone-guidelines)
- **Bags**: Size limits; cloakrooms may be available
- **Food/drink**: Often not allowed inside
- **Smoking**: Not permitted

Leave non-essentials at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust. See [luggage storage](/blog/ayodhya-luggage-storage-lockers-near-ram-mandir). Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number).
