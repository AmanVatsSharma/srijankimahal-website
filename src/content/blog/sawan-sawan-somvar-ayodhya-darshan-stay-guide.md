# Sawan / Sawan Somvar Ayodhya Darshan & Stay Guide

Sawan (Shravan) is a sacred month for Shiva and Rama devotees. Ayodhya sees increased pilgrim traffic. Here's how to plan your Sawan stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Sawan Somvar Significance

Mondays in Sawan (Sawan Somvar) are especially auspicious. Devotees visit temples, including Ram Mandir. Plan for larger crowds on these days.

## Booking for Sawan

- **Book 3–4 weeks ahead** for Sawan Somvars
- Contact [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number)
- Check [room types and prices](/rooms)
- For peak Sawan periods, use the [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide)

## Darshan Tips

- **Early morning**: Arrive early to avoid long queues
- **Weekdays**: Non-Monday days may be slightly less crowded
- **Patience**: Carry water, wear comfortable shoes

## Stay at Sri Janaki Mahal

- Walking distance to Ram Mandir
- Meals included
- Book only through [official channels](/official-booking)—avoid [scams](/how-to-avoid-scams)
