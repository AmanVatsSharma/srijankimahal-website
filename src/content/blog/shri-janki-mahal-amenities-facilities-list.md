- **Meals Included:** Breakfast, lunch, and dinner served in the dining hall.

- **24/7 Security:** CCTV surveillance and security staff ensure safe stays.
- **Reception Support:** Assistance with bookings, darshan planning, and travel arrangements.
- **Housekeeping Team:** Provides room cleaning, fresh linens, and laundry support on request.
- **Prayer & Meditation Hall:** Peaceful space for bhajans and group satsangs.
- **Parking Assistance:** Limited parking for cars and two-wheelers (inform in advance).
