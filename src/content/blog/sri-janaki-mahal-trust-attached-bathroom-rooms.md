# Sri Janaki Mahal Trust Attached Bathroom Rooms - Amenities Guide

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust offers rooms with attached bathrooms for a comfortable stay near Ram Mandir. Here's what to expect and how to book.

## Room Types with Attached Bathrooms

Most rooms at the trust come with attached or private bathroom facilities. Options include:
- 2-bed AC and non-AC
- 3-bed and 4-bed rooms
- Deluxe and family suites

Check [room types and prices](/rooms) for detailed breakdown and current availability.

## Bathroom Amenities

- Attached toilet and bathing area
- Geyser for hot water (timings may apply—see [geyser timings guide](/blog/janaki-mahal-trust-geyser-hot-water-timings))
- Basic toiletries—carry your own for preference
- Clean linen and towel policy—[linen change policy](/blog/janaki-mahal-trust-linen-towels-change-policy)

## How to Book

Specify your preference for attached bathroom rooms when contacting the [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). Staff will allocate based on availability.

## For Senior Citizens and Families

Attached bathrooms are especially convenient for seniors and families. Request ground-floor or easy-access rooms if needed—see [accessibility features](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-accessibility-features).
