# Sri Janaki Mahal Trust Spiritual Ambience & Satsang

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust offers a devotional environment for pilgrims. Here's what to expect regarding spiritual ambience and satsang.

## Spiritual Ambience

The trust operates as a dharmshala—a place for pilgrims. The setting is simple and focused on devotion. Located near Ram Mandir, it supports a pilgrimage mindset.

## Satsang and Group Activities

- **Availability**: The trust may host or permit satsang and bhajan—inquire when you [book](/official-booking)
- **Timing**: Usually evening or early morning
- **Participation**: Guests are welcome to join when programs are held

**Exact schedule** varies—contact the [official number](/contact-number) for current activities.

## Individual Practice

- **Quiet time**: See [quiet meditation space](/blog/sri-janaki-mahal-trust-quiet-meditation-space)
- **Room**: Your room offers privacy for personal prayer and reflection

## Community

Fellow guests are often pilgrims. The shared purpose creates a supportive atmosphere. Respect others’ space and follow trust rules.

## Booking

Book via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact](/contact-number). See [rooms](/rooms) and [location](/location).
