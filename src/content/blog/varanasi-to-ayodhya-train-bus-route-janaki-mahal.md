# Varanasi to Ayodhya Train & Bus Route - Sri Janaki Mahal Stay Plan

Combining Kashi and Ayodhya in one pilgrimage is common. This guide covers travel from Varanasi to Ayodhya and how to reach Sri Janaki Mahal Trust.

## Train Options

**Direct trains**: Several trains connect Varanasi Junction (BSB) and Ayodhya Junction (AY). Journey time is roughly 4–6 hours.
- Check IRCTC or railway enquiry for current timings
- Book in advance during festivals

**Route**: Varanasi → Sultanpur → Ayodhya (approximately)

## Bus Options

**UPSRTC and private buses**: Regular services run between Varanasi and Ayodhya. Journey 5–7 hours depending on traffic.
- Buses may terminate at Ayodhya bus stand or near Ram Mandir
- Book ahead for festival dates

## From Ayodhya Junction to Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

Sri Janaki Mahal Trust is in Karsewakpuram, close to Ram Mandir. From Ayodhya Junction:
- **Auto/rickshaw**: About 15–20 minutes to Karsewakpuram
- **Walking**: Possible if you have light luggage; ~2–3 km

See the [location from Ayodhya Junction guide](/location/from-ayodhya-junction) for detailed directions.

## Suggested Itinerary

1. **Day 1**: Varanasi darshan, overnight in Varanasi
2. **Day 2**: Travel to Ayodhya (train or bus), [book room at Janaki Mahal](/official-booking) in advance
3. **Day 3–4**: Ayodhya darshan, stay at Sri Janaki Mahal Trust

Book your Ayodhya stay early via [official booking](/official-booking) or [contact number](/contact-number). Check [room types](/rooms) and [festival booking guide](/festival-booking-guide) for peak periods.
